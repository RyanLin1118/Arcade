Questions about our Games

How do you play the games on our site?

You need to have something to click on like a mouse and arrows on your keyboard to control certain games. Most importantly, you need a computer to play the games.

What was the purpose of making these games?

We wanted to create games from an online arcade so gamers could play games for free. On your games, we don't have a log in button, so your progress in the games that you played might not be saved. The next day you play, you have to restart your progress.

What are some types of games in our online arcade?

We have a variety of game in our online arcade. Some types of games are arcade games, 2-d platform games, and many more other games.

How are the games in our website going to satisfy parents?

There are some games that are educational that teaches players school subjects like math games. Not all our games are just foscued on shooter games or other non-educational games. This will help get parents' approval.
